Privacy Policy

Effective: 22 June 2025

This notice explains how GoAISite collects and uses your data.

Data We Collect

  • Account info: name, email, encrypted password or Google OAuth ID.
  • Payment data: Paddle transaction ID, plan level, billing country (we never see full card details).
  • Prompt inputs: the text you submit to generate websites.
  • Usage analytics: IP address, device, and pages visited (via Plausible).

Third-Party Processors

GoAiSite uses the following providers solely to deliver the service:

  • Paddle.com – Merchant-of-Record for payments and invoicing.
  • Chatislav.ai – Support chat widget.
  • Google LLC – OAuth sign-in and font hosting.

How We Use Data

  • Provide and improve the service.
  • Process payments and prevent fraud.
  • Email transactional notices.

Your Rights

You may access, correct, or delete personal data by emailing support@goaisite.com. EU/EEA users can lodge complaints with their DPA.

Cookies

GoAISite uses essential cookies for login sessions and Stripe Checkout only.
