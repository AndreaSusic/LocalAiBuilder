Refund Policy

At GoAISite, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not happy, you may request a full refund within 14 days of your purchase date.

How to Request a Refund

  1. Contact us at support@goaisite.com with your order details.
  2. Include your Paddle Order ID and the reason for your request.
  3. We will process your refund within 5 business days of approval.

This policy applies to all subscription plans purchased through Paddle.
