Cookie Policy

This page explains what cookies and similar technologies we use on goaisite.com and why. For details on how we handle personal data, please see our Privacy Policy.

1. What Are Cookies & Similar Tech?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by your browser. We also use local storage (a browser feature that stores data indefinitely) to remember certain preferences.

2. Cookies & Storage Items We Use

A. Strictly Necessary

Name / KeyProviderExpiryPurpose
goai_session First-party (GoAISite) End of session Keeps you logged in and secures your account.
cookie_consent First-party 6 months Saves your cookie-banner choice so we don't ask again every visit.
__paddle_session Paddle (checkout) 30 minutes Processes payment and prevents fraud.
paddlejs_checkout_country Paddle 30 minutes Pre-selects tax/VAT rules for your billing country.

B. Analytics

We use Plausible Analytics configured in cookie-less mode. No identifiers or persistent files are stored on your device; all metrics are aggregated and anonymised on EU servers.

C. Marketing / Advertising

None. GoAISite does not run cross-site tracking or ad pixels.

D. Local Storage Items

KeyExpiryPurpose
consent_preferences Until deleted Stores your granular cookie choices (functional, analytics).
builder_draft
(only when logged in)		 Until you publish or discard draft Saves unsaved website edits so you don't lose work if the tab closes.

3. How to Manage Cookies

  • Banner settings → Click the "Cookie Settings" link in our footer or banner to update your preferences at any time.
  • Browser controls → You can delete or block cookies via your browser settings; note that essential cookies are required for the platform to function.
  • California residents → If we ever add marketing cookies, the banner will display a "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" link as required by the CPRA.

4. Updates

We review this table at least once a year and whenever we add a new cookie. Changes will be posted here; material updates will be flagged in the banner for 30 days. Last updated on 30 June 2025.
