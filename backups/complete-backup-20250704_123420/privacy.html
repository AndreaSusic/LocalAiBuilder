3. How & With Whom We Share Data

We do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioural advertising. We only disclose data to vetted processors under strict contracts:

  • Paddle.com Market Ltd – Merchant-of-Record, payment & invoicing (UK)
  • Google LLC – OAuth sign-in, Google Fonts CDN (USA)
  • Chatislav.ai – Real-time support chat (EU data centre)
  • Plausible Analytics – First-party, cookie-less analytics (EU/EEA hosted)

Each provider is bound by a Data Processing Addendum (DPA) and may only process data on our instructions.

4. International Transfers

Where data leaves the EEA/UK (e.g., to Google LLC in the United States) we rely on the EU Standard Contractual Clauses ("SCCs") and UK Addendum, plus technical measures such as encryption in transit and at rest.

5. Retention Periods

  • Account & site content → kept while your subscription is active + 24 months.
  • Billing records → 7 years (tax/regulatory).
  • Prompt text → 30 days (rolling deletion).
  • Support tickets → 12 months after resolution.
  • Back-ups → erased within 30 days of retention period expiring.

6. Your Rights

EU/UK (GDPR): access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection, complaint to a supervisory authority.
California (CCPA/CPRA): know, delete, correct, opt-out of sale/share, limit the use of sensitive personal info.

To exercise any right, email privacy@goaisite.com or use the "Privacy Request" form linked in the footer. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Automated Decision-Making

GoAISite does not engage in automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects as defined by GDPR Art. 22.

8. Cookies & Tracking

  • Essential – Session cookie (goai_session) to keep you logged in.
  • Paddle – Checkout flow cookies (__paddle_session) for fraud prevention.
  • Analytics – Plausible operates without cookies; no personal data stored.
  • Marketing – None.

For granular details (names, expiry, purpose) see our separate Cookie Policy.

9. Updates to This Notice

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect legal or operational changes. We will post the new version here and, if the changes are material, notify you by email or in-app banner at least 14 days before they take effect. The "Last Updated" date at the top shows the current revision.
