Frequently Asked Questions

How long does Invisalign® take?

Most cases finish within 6-14 months, depending on complexity and how diligently you wear the trays.

Is it painful?

You may feel gentle pressure the first day of a new tray, but patients report far less discomfort than braces.

Will insurance cover Invisalign®?

Many orthodontic policies apply. We’ll check your benefits and offer flexible payment plans if needed.
