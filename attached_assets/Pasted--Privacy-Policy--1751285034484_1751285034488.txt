<h2>3. How & With Whom We Share Data</h2>
<p>

</p>
<ul>

<h1>Cookie&nbsp;Policy</h1>
<p>
  This page explains what cookies and similar technologies we use on
  <code>goaisite.com</code> and why.  For details on how we handle personal data,
  please see our <a href="/privacy">Privacy&nbsp;Policy</a>.
</p>

<hr />

<h2>1. What Are Cookies & Similar Tech?</h2>
<p>
  Cookies are small text files placed on your device by your browser.  
  We also use <em>local storage</em> (a browser feature that stores data
  indefinitely) to remember certain preferences.
</p>

<hr />
